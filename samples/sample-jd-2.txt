JOB DESCRIPTION
Senior Product Designer

Company: InteractiveFlow Inc.
Location: New York, NY (Hybrid)
Employment Type: Full-time
Salary Range: $130,000 - $160,000

ABOUT THE ROLE
InteractiveFlow Inc. is seeking a talented Senior Product Designer to lead design initiatives and create exceptional user experiences. You'll work on products used by millions of users worldwide, collaborating with product managers, engineers, and leadership.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design user-centered digital products and experiences
- Lead design thinking workshops and ideation sessions
- Conduct user research, interviews, and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Establish and maintain design systems and patterns
- Collaborate with engineering teams on implementation
- Advocate for design best practices and user needs
- Mentor junior designers and provide design feedback
- Analyze user behavior and metrics to improve designs
- Present design solutions to stakeholders
